13. Episode 3: Fracture Scan
Objective
• Complete the first mission arc started in Episodes 1 and 2
• Prove the universe can support a second product category beyond creator signal warfare
• Show Kage-9 applying forensic diagnostics to a damaged machine or vehicle system
• Expand the counterfeit lattice threat into the physical world
• Introduce TorqueShed naturally as a mechanical intelligence platform
• End with a broader franchise hook that supports future modules and missions
Story Function
Episode 3 shifts the war from pure signal-space into the machine world. The recovered system is TorqueShed, presented as a
forensic mechanical intelligence bay used to reconstruct failures, expose sabotage, and recover truth from damaged systems.
Scene 1: Signal Trail to Steel
Visual: The counterfeit lattice map from Episode 2 extends beyond campaign and signal hubs into a dead industrial sector.
One branch pulses inside a transport corridor where archive materials once moved between zones.
Voiceover / dialogue: Counterfeit systems never stop at perception.
If left alone, they move into infrastructure.
Scene 2: The Wrecked Transport Unit
Visual: A transport unit or armored courier vehicle sits crippled in an abandoned industrial zone. Burn marks, impact scoring,
and mechanical distortion suggest failure, but the pattern is wrong.
Voiceover / dialogue: Official reports said failure.
The machine said otherwise.
Scene 3: Silence in the Yard
Visual: The industrial yard is littered with dead cranes, frozen loaders, and broken rail systems. Kage-9 walks through the steel
graveyard.
Voiceover / dialogue: When machines die clean, the damage makes sense.
This one had been taught to lie.
Scene 4: Survivor Fragment
Visual: A wounded operator or scavenger from the transport route gives Kage-9 a fractured data shard or final route token.
Voiceover / dialogue: Survivor: It wasn’t cargo. It was a relay core.
Kage-9: Then someone wanted it to disappear.
Scene 5: Hidden Service Bay
Visual: Following the shard’s route key, Kage-9 finds a concealed maintenance bay built into the industrial ruins.
Voiceover / dialogue: The old network kept more than archives.
It kept ways to read what damage tried to hide.
Scene 6: TorqueShed Revealed
Visual: Inside the bay sits TorqueShed, a mechanical intelligence console surrounded by suspended scan arms, diagnostic
rails, and layered reconstruction displays.
Voiceover / dialogue: TorqueShed.
Built to reconstruct failure.
